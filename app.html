Weekly mock exams

Each paper mirrors the real test: 35 multiple-choice questions (A–E), 40-minute countdown, balanced across the five strands. Your best result per week is saved on this device.

Focused study

Each week's exam has its own focused study, built from the skills you got wrong in that week. For each skill you get a concept card (principle, method, worked example) and then brand-new "now you try" questions — same method, new numbers, often reversed (e.g. find the total instead of a share). The exam questions themselves are never repeated, so you're always tested on fresh problems. Skills are interleaved so you must pick the right method, and once mastered they come back for spaced review a few days later so they stick.
